#### [[Вопросы к Экзамену]]
---
$$\begin{align}
	&a\in\mathbb{Q}\\
	&a = (a_0;a_1,\ldots,a_n) \Leftrightarrow |\\
	&| \Leftrightarrow a= a_0+\frac{1}{a_1+\frac{1}{a_2+\frac{1}{\ldots}}}
\end{align}$$
$$\begin{align}
	a &= \frac{p}{q} \\
	\frac{p}{q} &= a_0 + \frac{1}{\alpha_0} \\
	\alpha_0 &= \frac{1}{\frac{p}{q} - a_0} = \frac{q}{p - qa_0}= a_1 + \frac{1}{\alpha_1} \\
	\quad \vdots \\
	\alpha_{n-1} &= \frac{1}{\alpha_{n-2}-a_{n-1}} = a_n
\end{align}$$
